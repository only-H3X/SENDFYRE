##victimdomain## – Processed domain of the recipient (special characters removed, converted to case).
##victimfulldomain## – Full, unprocessed domain of the recipient.
##victimname## – Processed name of the recipient (special characters removed, converted to case).
##victimemail## – Email address of the recipient.
##victimb64email## – Base64-encoded email of the recipient.
##victimb64emailnp## – Base64-encoded email of the recipient (no padding, trailing = removed).
##victimhexemail## – Hex-encoded email of the recipient (useful to avoid exposing raw or base64 email).
##victimdomainlogo## – HTML version of the recipient’s branded logo (used for branding or letterhead).
##victimrealdomain## – Real name of the recipient’s domain (fetched from a database).
##victimdomainlogosrc## – Base64-encoded value of the recipient’s domain logo.
##victimobfhexemail## – Obfuscated hex-encoded email of the recipient.
##victimb64domain## – Base64-encoded domain of the recipient.
##victimb64name## – Base64-encoded name of the recipient.
##myname## – Processed name of the sender (special characters removed, converted to case).
##mydomain## – Processed domain of the sender (special characters removed, converted to case).
##link## – Value of the link in the "LINK=" field in the config.
##linkb64## – Base64-encoded value of the link in the "LINK=" field in the config.
##qrcode## – QR code image of the link in the "LINK=" field in the config (usually used in an HTML tag).
##qrcodedata## – Base64-encoded QR code image of the link in the "LINK=" field in the config.
##date(n)## – Generates a date attribute.
##random(tag)## – Generates a random value for the given tag.
